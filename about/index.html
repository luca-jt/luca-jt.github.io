About

My name is Luca Thomas and I am a Computer Science student in Germany at the Technical University of Braunschweig. I am engaged in doing my Bachelors Degree at the moment, but alongside my studies at university, I am almost always working on something on the side privately. I decided to start this blog to showcase the work that I have been doing and enable others to quickly learn more about it. As I come along some interesting topics while working on said projects, I might write some articles about them here and there if I think that they might be helpful to other people. Sometimes, this will also have associations to things I am working on in an academic setting.

I am a both technical and creative person at heart and I want to pursue meaningful work. I am very much involved in the Game Development space as I see that domain as the best combination of both of these worlds. My main interest is to make the software world a less terrible place. I want to become the most competent person I can be and love to work on hard problems. I am constantly trying to learn as much as I can and I hope that never stops.

This website will contain content in both English and German language. I hope it helps other people quickly learn more about me and the work I am doing.
